How to Use

Welcome to our PGP encryption/decryption site! Our PGP (Pretty Good Privacy) system allows you to secure your messages and verify their authenticity.

Here's how to use our site:

  1. GENERATE A NEW KEY PAIR : In the "Creating new PGP keys pair" section, enter your name, email address, and choose a passphrase. Click on "GENERATE NEW PGP KEYS PAIR" to obtain a private key and a public key. Save these keys and NEVER SHARE YOUR PRIVATE KEY!

  2. ENCRYPT A MESSAGE : In the "Encryption" section, copy the recipient's public key into the designated field. Then, enter your message in the "Enter the text to be encrypted here" field and click on "ENCRYPT TEXT". The encrypted text will be displayed in the corresponding field. You can then copy or download it.

  3. DECRYPT A MESSAGE : In the "Decryption" section, paste your private key into the "Enter the private key here" field and enter your passphrase. Next, copy the encrypted text you received into the "Enter the text to be decoded here" field and click on "DECRYPT TEXT". The original text will be displayed in the corresponding field.

  4. SIGN AND VERIFY A MESSAGE : In the "Sign/Verify pgp message" section, paste your private key into the "Enter the private key here" field and enter your passphrase. Then, enter your message in the "Enter the text to be signed here" field and click on "SIGN TEXT". The signature will appear in the corresponding field. To verify a signature, paste the sender's public key and the signed message into the appropriate fields, then click on "VERIFY TEXT".

Feel free to use our PGP system to secure your communications and verify the authenticity of the messages you receive.

Security and Transparency: Your data is secure, and no information is ever saved during or after the process. For complete transparency, you can review the source code of this project, which is fully open-source, on GitHub. The sole purpose of this project is to help people communicate securely.

We hope you enjoy using our PGP site and that it enables you to communicate securely!
